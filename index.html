Cat Lounge Guidelines

  • Please be gentle and respectful with our cats
  • Wash your hands before and after interacting with the cats
  • Let the cats come to you — don't force interaction
  • No flash photography to avoid startling the cats
  • Food and drinks must be kept in designated areas
  • Children must be supervised by adults at all times

About Nox Cafe

Your neighborhood spot for coffee, beers, and billiards in Imus, Cavite.

Our Story

Nox Cafe was born from a simple idea: create a space where people can relax, connect, and enjoy life's simple pleasures. Whether you're a coffee enthusiast, a cat lover, or a billiards player, we've crafted a unique environment that brings together the best of all worlds.

Located in the heart of Imus, Cavite, we're more than just a cafe — we're a community hub where friends meet, memories are made, and every visit feels like coming home. From our carefully curated menu to our adorable cat companions and professional billiards tables, every detail has been designed with you in mind.
